How to Don

DONNING
SEQUENCE

Step 1
INSPECT Suit
Check outer layer, visor, seams, and moisture barrier for damage before every deployment. Never don a damaged suit.
Step 2
DON SCBA
Put on and test SCBA breathing apparatus first — before donning the proximity suit over it.
Step 3
WEAR Suit
Step into suit legs, insert arms. Partner assists with rear fastening and hood alignment over SCBA mask.
Step 4
CHECK & GO
Partner confirms: visor locked, no gaps at wrist/ankle. Confirm radio comms. Approach only with charged hose backup.

⚠ Critical: NEVER approach a proximity fire without SCBA. Always operate in pairs. Post-use: inspect for damage before returning to storage. Never fold — store hanging to protect aluminized coating. Re-certify every 3 years. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
