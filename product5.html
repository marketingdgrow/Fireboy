Operation

HOW TO
OPERATE

01
SWING the Arm
Swing the hose reel arm toward the fire. It rotates up to 180° — point it toward the affected area.
02
RUN out the Hose
Pull the hose to the required length. The reel drum spins freely — run as much hose as needed.
03
OPEN the Valve
Turn the control valve (usually at the reel inlet) to open water supply to the hose.
04
ATTACK the Fire
Squeeze pistol grip nozzle. Use jet for distance or spray for close-range suppression. Aim at base of fire.

⚠ Important: Never use a water hose reel on electrical fires or flammable liquid fires. Use appropriate dry powder or CO₂ extinguisher instead. Keep exit behind you. If fire is large or spreading, evacuate immediately. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
