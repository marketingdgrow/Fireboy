Operation

HOW IT
ACTIVATES

Step 1
FIRE STARTS
Heat rises from the fire source and accumulates at ceiling level around the sprinkler head.
Step 2
BULB HEATS
The glass bulb containing heat-sensitive liquid expands as ambient temperature approaches 68°C.
Step 3
BULB BURSTS
At exactly 68°C the bulb shatters, releasing the valve cap and opening the water orifice.
Step 4
WATER DISCHARGES
Pressurised water flows through the orifice, deflects off the pendant plate into a cone spray pattern.

⚠ Critical: NEVER paint, coat, or wrap any sprinkler head — this prevents activation. Maintain 450mm clear space below every head at all times. Replace any activated head immediately — never re-use a discharged sprinkler. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
