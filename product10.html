Operation

PRE-USE
DONNING CHECK

Step 1
CHECK Cylinder
Verify cylinder pressure > 200 bar. Check cylinder valve, body, and harness for damage before donning.
Step 2
MASK Seal Test
Don mask, close bypass valve, inhale — mask should collapse slightly confirming airtight seal.
Step 3
OPEN Valve
Open cylinder valve fully. Confirm HUD display shows correct pressure and demand valve responds on inhalation.
Step 4
DON & Deploy
Fit harness, tighten all buckles. Confirm radio comms. Enter only with a buddy — never alone.

⚠ Critical: Use ONLY Grade E breathing quality compressed air — industrial air is potentially fatal. Never enter alone — always use buddy system. At 75 bar alarm: begin immediate exit. Cylinder hydrostatic test every 5 years. Retire carbon cylinders at 15 years regardless of condition. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
