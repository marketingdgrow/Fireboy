Operation

HOW TO
OPERATE — PASS

01
PULL the Pin
Remove the safety pin. The extinguisher is safe to handle — unlike CO₂, the horn does not freeze.
02
AIM at the Base
Aim the nozzle at the BASE of the fire. The agent works by chemical chain interruption, not cooling.
03
SQUEEZE the Lever
Squeeze the handle. HFC-236fa discharges as a clear, odourless gas — you will see a slight mist.
04
SWEEP & VERIFY
Sweep at the base until extinguished. The agent dissipates in seconds — no cleanup, no residue.

⚠ Important: While HFC-236fa has a safe exposure concentration (NOAEL 10%), avoid deliberate inhalation. After discharge in a very small enclosed space, ventilate briefly before re-entry. Always weigh the cylinder quarterly — refill when weight loss exceeds 5%. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
