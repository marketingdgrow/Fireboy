Maintenance

TESTING
SCHEDULE

Weekly
WALK-TEST
Test one zone per week using test key. Confirm alarm LED, sounder activation, and panel reset.
Monthly
LOG REVIEW
Check 100-event log for nuisance alarms — indicates a detector requiring cleaning or replacement.
Annual
FULL TEST
Test all zones, all detectors, battery load test, relay output, and sounder verification.
3–4 Yrs
BATTERY
Replace sealed lead-acid battery every 3–4 years regardless of apparent condition.

⚠ Important: Never disable or isolate zones permanently without a written hot-work or maintenance permit. Battery failure may go unnoticed until a mains outage — annual load testing is critical. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
