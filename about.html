FAQ

Common Fire Safety Questions

Quick answers for customers planning new installations, upgrades, or maintenance.

Which fire extinguisher is suitable for my building?

It depends on the risk type. Offices usually need ABC extinguishers, while kitchens, server rooms, and industrial areas may need specialized units.

Do you provide installation and refilling services?

Yes. We support product supply, installation guidance, extinguisher refilling, inspection, and annual maintenance contracts.

How often should fire safety equipment be checked?

Basic visual checks should happen regularly, and professional inspection or maintenance should be scheduled at least once a year.

Can you help with bulk orders for businesses?

Yes. We can recommend product quantities, organize delivery, and support commercial or industrial fire safety requirements.
