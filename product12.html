Operation

HOW TO
OPERATE — PASS

Step 1
PULL the Pin
Pull safety pin from handle. Breaks tamper seal and unlocks the operating lever. Keep exit behind you.
Step 2
AIM at Base
Direct the nozzle at the BASE of the fire — for Class F, aim at the near edge of burning oil surface.
Step 3
SQUEEZE Lever
Squeeze the handle firmly. Superheated water mist begins discharging — 60–90 seconds of continuous agent.
Step 4
SWEEP & WITHDRAW
Sweep nozzle side to side at base. Once extinguished, withdraw and watch for re-ignition before turning away.

⚠ Important: Do NOT use standard water extinguishers on Class F fires — violent splattering will occur. This water MIST unit is specifically designed and rated for Class F. After any use, recharge immediately — even partial discharge. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
