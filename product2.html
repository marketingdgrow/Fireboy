Operation

HOW TO
OPERATE — PASS

01
PULL the Pin
Pull the locking pin. Never hold the horn — it becomes extremely cold (-79°C) during discharge.
02
AIM at the Base
Aim the horn directly at the BASE of the fire. Stand 2–3 meters away from the fire source.
03
SQUEEZE the Lever
Squeeze the operating lever firmly. CO₂ gas is discharged under high pressure as a cold white cloud.
04
SWEEP Side to Side
Move the horn in a sweeping motion at the base of the fire until extinguished. Ventilate the area after use.

⚠ Critical Safety Warning: NEVER hold the CO₂ horn directly — it reaches -79°C and can cause severe frostbite. Do not use in confined spaces without ventilation — CO₂ displaces oxygen and can cause asphyxiation. Always evacuate the space immediately after discharge and ventilate thoroughly before re-entry.
