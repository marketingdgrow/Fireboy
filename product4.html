Installation

INSTALLATION
GUIDELINES

01
Location & Height
Mount on each floor landing, 1.0–1.2 meters above finished floor level. Locate near staircase lobby as per NBC 2016 clause 4.4.
02
Thread Connection
Connect inlet to wet riser using BSP parallel thread with PTFE tape. Ensure no cross-threading. Torque to manufacturer spec.
03
Pressure Test
Hydrostatic test at 24 bar for 30 minutes after installation. Check for leaks at valve body, gland, and connections.
04
Hose Cabinet
Install in dedicated fire hose cabinet with glass-fronted door, breakable in emergency. Red colour coding mandatory.
05
Signage
Post IS:12349 compliant fire hydrant signage above cabinet. Direction arrows required in corridors per local fire authority.
06
Annual Maintenance
Operate valve quarterly to prevent seizure. Replace gland packing every 3 years. Full flow test annually with fire authority.

⚠ Important: Always keep blanking cap fitted when valve is not in use — debris ingress can block the valve at a critical moment. All hydrant system work must be performed by a licensed fire contractor. System must be registered with local Fire Authority before commissioning. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
