Operation

HOW TO
OPERATE — PASS

01
PULL the Pin
Pull the safety pin from the handle. This breaks the tamper seal and unlocks the operating lever.
02
AIM at the Base
Point the hose/nozzle at the BASE of the fire — not the flames. Aiming at flames wastes agent.
03
SQUEEZE the Lever
Firmly squeeze the top handle or lever. This releases the pressurized dry powder agent.
04
SWEEP Side to Side
Sweep the nozzle side to side at the base of the fire until it is fully extinguished.

⚠ Important: After any use — even partial — the extinguisher must be professionally recharged before returning to service. Always keep exit behind you when operating. Do not re-enter an area immediately after using dry powder. If the fire re-ignites or grows, evacuate and call emergency services immediately. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
