How to Install

INSTALLATION
GUIDE

Step 1
PLAN Layout
Map all emergency exit routes. Place a sign at every decision point — junction, corner, and above each exit door.
Step 2
CLEAN Surface
Clean mounting surface with isopropyl alcohol. Ensure surface is dry and dust-free before applying adhesive.
Step 3
FIX Sign
Peel backing from 3M adhesive and press firmly for 30 seconds. For masonry, use pre-drilled holes and appropriate wall plugs.
Step 4
TEST Glow
After installation, photograph all signs in complete darkness after 60-minute charge to verify glow performance.

⚠ Important: Signs must be at eye level (1.8–2.2m) or above exit doors. Verify glow performance annually in complete darkness. Replace any sign with glow duration below 4 hours. Maintain a signage register for NOC compliance. Emergency: 101 (Fire)
